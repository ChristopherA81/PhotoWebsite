My name is Christopher Armstrong. I am an American living in Berlin, Germany and also an amateur photographer. I began taking photgraphs in Fall of 2020 during the pandemic. My photographs are a documentation of the things I see on a daily basis here in Berlin. I use only an Olympus Trip 35 point and shoot camera. My subjects are mostly objects. My preferred film is Kodak Ultra-max and Portra. I also occasionally use Fuji Film 400.
